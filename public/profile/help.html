Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order? ▼
Browse products, add to cart, select delivery address, choose payment method and confirm order. You'll receive order confirmation via SMS and email.
How can I track my order? ▼
Go to Profile → My Orders → Select your order → Click "Track Order". You'll see real-time status updates.
What is the return policy? ▼
Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Go to My Orders → Return to initiate.
How do I create my shop? ▼
Profile → Create Shop → Fill shop details, select service type, add location → Submit. Admin will verify and approve within 24 hours.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, Wallets, and Cash on Delivery for eligible orders.
How do I cancel my order? ▼
Orders can be cancelled before shipping. Go to My Orders → Select order → Cancel. Refund will be processed in 5-7 business days.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Delivery time depends on your location and shop. Usually 30 mins to 2 hours for local delivery. You'll see estimated time at checkout.
How do I contact customer support? ▼
Use Live Chat, Call +91 1234567890, Email support@samanlive.com, or WhatsApp us. We're available 9 AM to 9 PM, 7 days a week.
